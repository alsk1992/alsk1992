### About

I design and ship **autonomous systems** ‚Äî AI agents, HFT engines, and execution infrastructure that trade, scrape, arbitrage, and operate across chains and platforms without human intervention. Full stack from bare metal Rust low-latency systems to polished React UIs. Specialist in **high-frequency trading**, market making, and building things that make money while I sleep.

**Currently:** Building [CloddsBot](https://github.com/alsk1992/CloddsBot) ‚Äî open source AI trading terminal operating across Polymarket, Binance, Hyperliquid, Solana DEXs, and 5 EVM chains. 1000+ market integrations, sub-second execution.
